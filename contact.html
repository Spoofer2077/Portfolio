Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a complex application could take 3-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your project requirements.

How do you handle revisions?

I include multiple revision rounds in my project scope to ensure we're aligned. Additional revisions beyond the agreed scope can be accommodated at an hourly rate.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Absolutely! I've worked with clients worldwide. We can communicate through video calls, email, and project management tools to keep everything on track.

What does your design process include?

My process typically includes: Discovery & Research, Wireframing, Visual Design, Prototyping, and Handoff. Each phase involves collaboration and feedback to ensure we're moving in the right direction.
